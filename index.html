杯水喻人生
The Cup of Water: An Analogy for Life

关键词：一体；差别；因果；欲望；平衡；人心

Keywords：Oneness; Difference; Karma; Desire; Balance; Human Heart

假设世界最初，是一杯完全静止的水。

Suppose the world began as a perfectly still cup of water.

静止，就没有分别；

Stillness means no distinction;

没有分别，就没有你我。

No distinction means no you and no me.

水动了，倾斜了一下。

Then the water moved and tilted a little.

于是有了多的一面，少的一面。

There came a fuller side and an emptier side.

我把少的一面叫 “我”，把多的一面叫 “世界”。

I called the emptier side "me", and the fuller side "the world".

以为我是我，世界是世界。

thinking I was me, and the world was the world.

但水依然还是同一杯水。

But the water was still the same cup.

人之所以有烦恼，是因为明明得到了，

Suffering arises because even when we have gained,

却还摆脱不了 “失去” 的概念。

we cannot let go of the idea of "loss".

因为水的倾斜，本就违背了它原本静止的特性。

For the tilting of the water goes against its original stillness.

头脑认为：我们是分开的。

The mind thinks: we are separate.

执着 “我是我，他是他”。

Clinging to "I am I, he is he".

但心是不会变的，一直在提醒我们：本是一体。

But the heart never changes, always reminding us: we are one.

人痛苦，不是因为世界错了，

Human suffering is not because the world is wrong,

而是头脑在分裂，心却合一。

but because the mind divides, while the heart unites.

头脑会忘，心永远不会。

The mind can forget, but the heart never will.

它一直提醒我们：

It keeps reminding us:

水，还是那一杯。

The water is still that same cup.

佛家所言 “本无分别、自性清净”，

Buddhism says: "No distinction inherently, pure original nature."

道家所讲 “道生混沌，复归无极”，

Taoism teaches: "The Tao gives birth to chaos, returning to the infinite."

圣经说 “上帝是一体，万物皆归于一”。

The Bible states: "God is one, and all things are united in Him."

我说这杯水 “一体为本、分裂为扰”，

I say of this cup: "Oneness is the root, division is disturbance."

都在说：世间本无对立，烦恼源于分别。

All speak the same: no opposition in the world, suffering arises from distinction.

这个世界很多人都觉得没因果，大多数人觉得都对，

Many in this world think there is no karma, and most believe they are right,

因为大多数人都只认现象层的东西；

for they only recognize things on the phenomenal level.

但因果不单单就在现象层。

But karma exists not merely on the phenomenal level.

那因果从现实、客观的角度讲成不成立？

Does karma hold objectively from a realistic perspective?

我觉得绝对成立，而且是必然成立。

I firmly believe it does, absolutely and inevitably.

那什么叫因果呢？

What is karma?

记住这个口诀：

First, remember this mantra:

本就无二、何来因果、当下因当下果。

Originally there is no duality; wherefore comes cause and effect? The cause of the present moment is the effect of the present moment.

我现在一句话给你们讲明白什么叫因果：

Let me make it clear in one sentence:

因好理解，因为它是在现象层；

The cause is easy to understand, for it lies in the phenomenal layer.

但是果呢，很多人认为是将来以后发生的事情，其实并不是，

As for the effect, many people think it is something that happens in the future, but this is not true.

果在本源层，在心里，不在现象里。

The effect lies in the origin layer, in the heart, not in the phenomenal world.

我为什么说有因果，而且也必然成立，

The reason I say karma exists and is inevitable,

原因很简单：

is simple:

因为本源层的果也可以变成现象层的因，

because the effect in the origin layer can become the cause in the phenomenal layer,

然后现象层的因又可以变成本源层的果。

and the cause in the phenomenal layer can in turn become the effect in the origin layer.

简单讲个道理：比如我当下拿了别人一个面包，

To put it simply: Suppose I take a piece of bread from someone right now,

如果它是现象层的因；

this is the cause on the phenomenal level.

而我心中那个 “他少了一个面包” 的感受、这个概念，就叫本源层的果。

The feeling and thought in my heart that "he has one less piece of bread" is the effect on the origin layer.

反过来，心中的这个概念、感受 (果)，

In turn, this inner thought and feeling (the effect),

又可以决定你下一步的选择 (因)，循环往复，就叫因果。

can determine your next choice (the cause). This cycle repeats endlessly, and this is called karma.

什么叫欲望？

What is desire?

我们一直觉得自己少了什么、空、缺、不安、却说不出来

We always feel we are missing something—empty, lacking, uneasy—but cannot name it.

于是本能去追求好的，要拥有，要弥补

So we instinctively pursue what is good, seek to possess, to fill the void.

但越补越缺，越补欲望越疯狂。

Yet the more we fill, the emptier we become; the more we chase, the wilder desire grows.

为什么？

Why?

因为都补错地方了。

Because we are filling the wrong place.

欲望不是要你补外面的东西

Desire is not asking you to fill yourself with external things.

欲望是人心平衡机制在提醒，

Desire is the reminder from the heart’s mechanism of balance.

欲望的来源是 人心的平衡机制，平衡机制来源于一体效应。

Its source is the broken connection to oneness—the heart has lost its balance.

欲望不是要你补 “身”

Desire is not asking you to fill the body.

是要你补 “心”

It is asking you to heal the heart.

不是补 “拥有”

Not by acquiring.

是补 “失去”

but by reclaiming what was lost.

钱、情、物、面子、成就，永远补不满。

Money, affection, things, pride, achievement—they can never fill the void; the more you take, the thirstier you get.

往心里补，回归平衡、恢复链接，欲望自然平息。

Fill the heart: return to balance, restore the connection—and desire will naturally subside.

为什么人永远不知足？

Why are people never satisfied?

为什么越追求越痛苦？

Why does more pursuit bring more suffering?

为什么所有外在满足都是暂时的？

Why is all external fulfillment only temporary?

因为欲望的根不在外面，在心里。

Because the root of desire is not outside, but in the heart.

欲望不是索取的信号，

Desire is not a signal to take.

是回家的提醒。

It is a reminder to return home.

因为我们自始至终都是同一杯水。

For we have always been, and will always be, the same cup of water.
